About Us :

Started in 1989 with the primary objective of imparting education mainly to minorities. The School is recognized by West Bengal Board Secondary Education as English Medium, co-ed and high school

The aim of the institution is to mould the students in a manner so that a they are imbued with the four characteristics which form the basis of an ideal character: Vitality, Courage, Sensitiveness, and Intelligence and to teach them to make national greatness the supreme purpose of education

With above aim , St. Michael Academy ventured into the domain of professional courses by offering tutorials for :

  1. WBCS (Exe.)
  2. SSC
  3. PSC
  4. WBJEE (Eng & Medical)
  5. CLAT/CU-LLB
  6. BANK (P.O and Clerk)
  7. Railways etc

Our evening classes are very advantageous for professionals and students who otherwise are engaged during day

In our constant endeavour to hone young minds for brighter tomorrow, SMILE has branched into degree and vocational courses which offer immense career opportunities

SMILE has recently has signed an MoU with Southern Institute of Medical Science (SIMS group of Institutions), by virtue of which we are now in position to offer the following courses

The new degree and vocational courses introduced by SMILE:

a) B.Sc  - Biotech

     Bioinformatics

     Genetics

b) B.Ed

All above degree courses are by U.G.C recognized University

c) GNM (General Nursing Midwifery)

d) Vocational Courses

- M.L.T (Medical Lab Technology)
- Nursing
- X-ray Technology

Uniqueness of Bio-Science Degree Courses at SMILE is that the students will have the choice of simultaneously preparing themselves for IELTS (International English Language Testing System) and G.R.E(General) , which will facilitate their ambition for further studies abroad ,as well as preparing all competitive exams (banks,wbcs,psc etc)

Here it is interesting to note that B.Sc Genetics is only offered by one college in Madyamgram, B.Sc Bioinformatics is not offered by any colleges B.Sc Biotechnology is offered by very few colleges in Kolkata,

 

Where the seats are few and cut-off percentage high

With the number of student going up every year and with dream and ambition soaring, the paucity of colleges offering the above courses leaves many young dreams shattered

Future Outlook

We wish to get affiliated with West Bengal State University for various courses they offer with an eye towards their Post Graduate Courses, so that St Michael’s Academy and SMILE together becomes a K.G to P.G institution
